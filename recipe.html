The Best Grilled Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Description

Best/Simplest ham and cheese sandwich you ever made.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Sliced Ham
  • Slice Cheese
  • mayonnaise
  • Dill Pickle
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Melt some butter in pan
  2. put both bread into the pan on low-midium heat until one side of the bread is golden brown
  3. Lay both slice of bread next to each other on a cutting board, crispy side down.
  4. With butter knife spread mayonnaise on to one side of one of the bread.
  5. 1 Slice Cheese on top of the mayonnaise.
  6. 2-3 slice of ham on top of the sliced cheese.
  7. Dill Pickles on top of the sliced ham.
  8. Bring the other slice of bread on top of the pickles
